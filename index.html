🔍 The "Rockets & Feathers" Loophole

Domestic retail prices in India display a pricing delay known by economists as the **"Rockets and Feathers"** effect. When international crude costs spike, retail prices respond like rockets, immediately scaling peaks. When crude costs fall, retail prices float down slowly like feathers, or remain frozen altogether.

This allows State-owned Oil Marketing Companies (OMCs) to recapture profit margins and rebuild their reserves by absorbing the windfall. The consumer effectively acts as an interest-free insurance fund for these oil corporations.
